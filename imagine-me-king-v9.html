About

The Story

Imagine Me King is a genre-blending duo from Dayton, Ohio, built on years of creative chemistry between Mike and Smitty. After their former band dissolved during college, the two shifted gears and found new purpose in an evolving sound, crafting lush, layered soundscapes and powerful dual vocals.

Known for their seamless integration of live instrumentation, they bring precision and artistry to every performance — often performing with headphones on stage to stay locked into their unique sonic world.
